Combat Tips

  1. Dash Attacks: Attack while moving to perform a dash attack for increased damage and knockback.
  2. Sword Tip Hits: Hit enemies with the tip of your sword for critical damage.
  3. Combo System: Chain attacks for increased experience rewards.
  4. Watch Your Health: Use heal ability when your health is low.
  5. Gems: Use gems wisely to choose bonus upgrades after leveling up.
